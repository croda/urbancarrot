Do you want to be a part of the green change in your city?

What?

Urban heat island is a phenomenon that refers to urban and suburban areas which have a higher temperature than their surroundings. As cities are growing, more green surface is being replaced by concrete buildings (and asphalt roads).

Our goal is to inform the community and engage it in the process of re-greening our environment.

Consequences

Energy Consumption

High energy demands for cooling and adds pressure on electricity grid during period of demand.

Air Quality

Increasing the energy demands lead to higher levels of air pollution and GHG emissions, also warmer air accelerates chemical reactions that produce ground-level ozone, or smog.

Human Health &Comfort
